Tom Yum Goong ต้มยำกุ้ง

Thailand | By Aisha Tan

Tom yum goong (sometimes spelled tom yum gung) is a light and delicious Thai soup made with shrimp that is hot and sour. Some refer to this soup as Lemongrass Soup, and to many, this is the dish that makes Thai cuisine world-famous. Tom yum goong (ต้มยำกุ้ง) is a variation of our Thai tom yum soups. In Thai, the word, "tom," means either soup or to boil, while, "yum,' means salad or to mix, and "goong," means shrimp or prawn.

Recipe Ingredients

  • 300g / 100z whole prawns / shrimps
  • 3 cup (750 ml) water
  • 1/2 cup (125 ml) chicken stock/broth
  • 2 stalks of lemongrass
  • 1.5 cm / 3/5" piece of galangal
  • 5 kaffir lime leaves
  • 2 Thai or birdseye chillies
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 120g / 40z oyster mushrooms
  • 1 roma tomato
  • 1/2 white onion
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 3 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 3 tablespoon lime juice
  • Coriander/cilantro

Recipe Instruction

  1. Peel the prawns. Place heads and shell in pot, reserve meat.
  2. Use a meat mallet or similar to bash the garlic, chilli and lemongrass so they burst open to release flavour. Add into pot.
  3. Crush kaffir lime leaves with your hands. Add into pot.
  4. Add galangal, stock and water. Bring to simmer on high heat, cover, then reduce to medium and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Strain the broth, discard the prawn shells etc, then return broth into same pot over low heat.
  6. Add onions and mushrooms, simmer 3 minutes. Add tomatoes, simmer for 1 minute.
  7. Stir in sugar and fish sauce, simmer for 1 minute.
  8. Add lime juice, then taste. Adjust sweet (sugar), salt (fish sauce) and sour (lime) to your taste.
  9. Ladle into bowls and serve with fresh coriander and fresh chilli.

Comment From ME

Tom Yum Goong is a delightful explosion of flavors! The perfect balance of spicy, sour, and savory with a hint of sweetness makes this soup a true Thai classic.
The combination of fresh herbs, succulent shrimp, and the rich, aromatic broth leaves a lasting impression. It’s a dish that warms the soul and excites the taste buds with every spoonful!
