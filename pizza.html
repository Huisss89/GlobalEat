Pizza

Italy | By Sophia Rossi

Pizza is one of the most popular foods in the world. It's based on a simple idea: a flatbread with toppings baked to high temperatures and consumed without utensils. The simplicity and range of topping options make pizza a quick and easy supper.

Recipe Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • Fine sliced mushrooms
  • Pepperoni
  • 2 teaspoon dry yeast
  • 100g (0.2 lbs) sheredded mozerella cheese
  • 2 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 medium size tomato (grated)
  • 2 teaspoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoon sugar

Recipe Instruction

  1. Mix the dry ingredients, flour, yeast, salt, and sugar.
  2. Start kneading as you slowly add warm water.
  3. Add the olive oil and continue kneading.
  4. Cover it and leave to stand at room temperature for around an hour to rise.
  5. Flatten the dough and put some flour.
  6. Transfer the pizza base on an oven tray.
  7. Mix grated tomato with tomato paste and dry (or fresh) basil.
  8. Spread it with the back of a spoon.
  9. Sprinkle 3/4 of the shredded mozzarella on the sauce.
  10. Put some sliced mushrooms and pepperoni on the sauce.
  11. Bake for approximately 25 to 30 degrees on 200C (400F) pre-heated oven.
  12. Sprinkle rest of the shredded mozerella and put it back to oven for a few more minutes.
